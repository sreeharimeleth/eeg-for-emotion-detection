About Our System

Our EEG-based emotion detection system processes brainwave data to identify emotional states using cutting-edge techniques such as Kalman filtering and Deep Belief Networks (DBN).

How It Works

The system preprocesses EEG data, extracts meaningful features, and classifies emotional states with the help of a trained DBN. Through this approach, the system can assist in personalized mental health care and human-computer interaction.
